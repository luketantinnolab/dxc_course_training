"Max Maeder is a Singaporean kitesurfer, born on September 12, 2006. He is the youngest winner in any Olympic sailing class competition, achieving this at the age of 16. Maeder won three consecutive Formula Kite Youth World Championships from 2021-2023 and won various open championships across Europe, Asia and Pan America. He won a bronze medal in the Men's Formula Kite event at the 2024 Summer Olympics in Paris. In addition to his native English, Maeder also understands Mandarin, German and French."
