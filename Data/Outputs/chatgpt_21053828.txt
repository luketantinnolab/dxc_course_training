Maximilian Maeder, also known as Max Maeder, is a Singaporean kitesurfer born on 12 September 2006. He is the reigning Formula Kite World, Asian, and European champion and is known as the youngest winner in any Olympic sailing class competition, achieving this feat at the age of 16. Maeder competed at the 2024 Summer Olympics in the inaugural Men's Formula Kite event where he won the bronze medal. Additionally, he is a three-time Formula Kite youth world champion from 2021 to 2023 and has won various open championships across Europe, Asia, and Pan America.
